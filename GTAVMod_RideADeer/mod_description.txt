<a href="http://gtaforums.com/topic/788343-vrel-script-hook-v/">ScriptHookV</a>

<ol>
	<li>If you have not already created the 'scripts' folder in your GTAV directory, do so now.</li>
	<li>Place the .CS file downloaded from this mod page into the 'scripts' folder.</li>
	<li>Run GTA, follow instructions below.</li>
</ol>

<b>Features</b>
<ul>
	<li>Ride on a deer. Yep. It's kinda funny.</li>
	<li>Changeable key binds!</li>
	<li>360 Controller support!</li>
	<li>Ride ANY deer you can get close enough to!</li>
</ul>
.
<u>Controls for keyboard (editable)</u>
<b>Activate/Deactivate</b>: NumPad7
<b>Spawn Deer/Ride</b>: NumPad4
<b>Get off Deer</b>: Tab
<b>Get off/on Deer</b>: Tab, F
<b>Navigate</b>: WASD & camera
<b>Run</b>: ENTER 
.
<u>Controls for Xbox 360 Controller</u>
<b>Get On/Off Deer</b>: Y
<b>Navigate</b>: LS and camera
<b>Run</b>: A
.
